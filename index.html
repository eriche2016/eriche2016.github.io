Biography
I am Jieru Zhao, a Ph.D. student from the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering at HKUST, where I am advised by Prof. Wei Zhang and working in Reconfigurable Computing Systems Lab. Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree from the School of Electronic Science and Engineering at Nanjing Univerity in 2015.
My research interests include but not limited to reconfigurable computing, electronic design automation (EDA) and embedded systems. My current work focuses on deploying DNNs on hardware efficiently.
